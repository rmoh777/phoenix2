Our Mission

Georgia Connects is dedicated to bridging the digital divide by helping eligible households access affordable internet and phone services. We believe that connectivity is essential for education, employment, healthcare, and community engagement.

Through our partnership with the Lifeline program, we provide guidance and support to help you navigate the application process and find the best service options for your needs.
